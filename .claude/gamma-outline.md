# Clover — Gamma Pitch Deck Outline

## Gamma Prompt

Create a 10-slide pitch deck for **Clover**, a co-working discovery app for builders and indie founders. The tone should be warm, aspirational, and direct — not corporate. Use a nature-inspired palette: deep forest green, soft lavender, and muted violet. Serif headings (Cormorant or similar), clean sans-serif body text.

---

## Slides

**Slide 1 — Hook**
Full-bleed dark slide. Large italic quote: *"You're sitting in a cafe. The person next to you is building something incredible. You'll never know."*

---

**Slide 2 — Founder story**
Headline: "The best work sessions I've had weren't solo."
Body: I love Shopify Builder Sunday — the energy, accountability, focus that comes from being around people who are building. But it's once a week. And in a group of 20, it's hard to truly lock in. The sweet spot? One or two people. Same energy. Working side by side.

---

**Slide 3 — The Problem**
Headline: "Solo builders are everywhere. They're just invisible to each other."
Two columns: (1) At cafes, libraries, co-working spaces across your city — people are heads-down on projects, startups, side hustles. Right next to you. (2) But we don't have a way to find each other. So we stay strangers. We work alone. We miss out on the people who get it.

---

**Slide 4 — The Insight**
Headline: "Small group coworking hits different."
Three bullet points: Accountability without the noise · Real conversation, not networking · You already share the same mindset

---

**Slide 5 — Product intro**
App name: Clover. Tagline: "Find your people. Do the work."
One-liner: Clover is a discovery app for builders. Set your work intent for today, see who's nearby and what they're building, connect and meet up.

---

**Slide 6 — How it works**
Three steps with icons: (1) Set your intent — what are you working on today, and where? (2) Browse nearby builders — see their projects, their vibe, their location. (3) Connect & meet up — mutual interest → match → meet IRL and get to work.

---

**Slide 7 — Product screens**
Show three mobile app screens: Discovery Feed, Profile Card, Match moment. Caption: Built and live on TestFlight.

---

**Slide 8 — Mirror moment**
Full-bleed dark slide. Large italic quote: *"If you've ever worked alone in a cafe and wished someone would just ask what you're building — this is for you."*

---

**Slide 9 — Where we are**
Headline: "Where we are"
Three status items: ✓ App built and live on iOS TestFlight · ✓ Friends who heard the idea immediately wanted in · → Looking for early beta users to shape what this becomes

---

**Slide 10 — Call to action**
Headline: "Be the first builders on Clover."
Large QR code. Subtext: Scan to join the beta waitlist.

---

## Tips for Gamma
- After generating, swap the theme to their darkest/moodiest preset — it'll match the forest green vibe better than the defaults
- Slides 1 and 8 work best as "statement" cards (Gamma's full-bleed text layout)
- Upload a screenshot of the app for slide 7 if you have one
