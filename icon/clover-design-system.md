# Clover — Brand Design System
*Last updated: March 2026*

---

## Brand Essence

**Name:** Clover  
**Tagline:** Show up together  
**Audience:** Gen Z and young millennials — builders, creators, remote workers  
**Positioning:** A coworking coordination app that helps friends and builders plan sessions together. Warm, social, and intentional — not productivity-tool serious.

---

## Logo Mark

### Concept
Four overlapping circles forming a clover shape, with the background colour punched through the centre. Flat, two-tone, no gradients, no shadows. Inspired by the restraint of Ditto and 222 — one shape, two colours, maximum confidence.

### Construction
- **Shape:** 4 circles arranged at cardinal points (top, right, bottom, left), overlapping at centre
- **Center cutout:** Background colour circle punched through the middle
- **Style:** Flat, geometric, no outlines, no detail

### Proportions (on 1024×1024 artboard)
| Element | Value |
|---|---|
| Artboard | 1024 × 1024 px |
| Petal radius | 226 px |
| Petal centre offset from middle | 215 px |
| Center cutout radius | 123 px |

### Clear Space
Always maintain a minimum clear space equal to the width of one petal around the full mark.

### Never Do
- Add gradients or shadows
- Outline the petals
- Use more than two colours
- Rotate the mark
- Stretch or distort proportions
- Place on a background that reduces contrast below AA accessibility standard

## Colour System

### Primary Palette

| Name | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lavender** | `#d8d0f0` | 216, 208, 240 | Icon background · primary bg · light surfaces |
| **Forest** | `#0c1f0e` | 12, 31, 14 | Icon mark · primary text · dark surfaces |

### Extended Palette

| Name | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Soft Lavender | `#ede8ff` | Light mode surfaces · subtle backgrounds |
| Warm Lavender | `#e8e0f8` | Cards · secondary surfaces |
| Deep Plum | `#1a0f2e` | Dark mode backgrounds |
| Violet | `#7c5cbf` | Accent · taglines · secondary text on light |
| Sage | `#5aaa55` | Success states · positive indicators |
| Forest Mid | `#1a3d1c` | Hover states · secondary dark surfaces |
| Forest Muted | `#3a6a3c` | Subtle text on dark backgrounds |

### Colour Rules
- **App icon:** Always `#d8d0f0` bg + `#0c1f0e` mark. Never swap or modify.
- **Dark surfaces:** Forest `#0c1f0e` or Deep Plum `#1a0f2e` backgrounds
- **Light surfaces:** Lavender `#d8d0f0` or Soft Lavender `#ede8ff`
- **Minimum contrast:** All text must meet WCAG AA (4.5:1 for body, 3:1 for large text)

## Typography

### Typefaces

| Role | Font | Weight | Style |
|---|---|---|---|
| Series name / display | Cormorant Garamond | Light 300 | Italic |
| Hero title | Cormorant Garamond | Regular 400 | Roman |
| UI / body / labels | DM Sans | Light 300 – Medium 500 | Roman |
| Episode / tertiary tags | DM Sans | Regular 400 | Roman |

### Hierarchy (Content / Video)
1. **"Coworking App Build Diary"** — Cormorant Garamond Italic Light, largest, dominant
2. **"Non-tech Girl"** — DM Sans small, secondary label
3. **"Ep. 01"** — DM Sans smallest, tertiary — this hierarchy must always be preserved

### Type Scale (UI)

| Level | Font | Size | Weight | Letter-spacing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Display | Cormorant Garamond | 48–64px | 300 | 0.02em |
| Heading | DM Sans | 22–28px | 400 | 0.05em |
| Wordmark | DM Sans | any | 300 | 0.28em |
| Body | DM Sans | 14–16px | 300–400 | 0 |
| Label / tag | DM Sans | 9–11px | 500 | 0.2em uppercase |
| Tagline | Cormorant Garamond | 11–13px | 300 | 0.1em italic |

## Logo Lockups

### Horizontal (primary)
Icon mark + wordmark side by side, vertically centred.  
Gap between icon and wordmark = icon height × 0.4.

### Stacked (secondary)
Icon mark centred above wordmark, tagline below.

### Icon only
Use for app icon, favicon, avatar, small placements below 40px.  
Never use wordmark-only without the mark except in editorial body text contexts.

### Approved Background Pairings

| Background | Mark colour | Wordmark colour |
|---|---|---|
| Forest `#0c1f0e` | Lavender petals `#d8d0f0` | Lavender `#d8d0f0` |
| Lavender `#d8d0f0` | Forest `#0c1f0e` | Forest `#0c1f0e` |
| Cream `#f5f2ec` | Forest `#0c1f0e` | Forest `#0c1f0e` |
| Deep Plum `#1a0f2e` | Lavender `#ede8ff` | Lavender `#ede8ff` |

## Voice & Tone

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| **Warm** | Friendly, social, not corporate |
| **Understated** | Confident without trying too hard |
| **Native** | Speaks the way the audience actually talks |
| **Honest** | Documents the journey, not the highlight reel |

### Vocabulary
- Say **"your crew"** not "your network"
- Say **"show up"** not "attend" or "join"
- Say **"session"** not "meeting" or "event"
- Say **"find your clover"** for moments of discovery/connection

---

## Content Aesthetic

**Guiding principle:** Polished but not trying too hard. Editorial restraint.

- **Video style:** Fast cuts (2–3s) for hook/context, hold (8–10s) on demo/CTA
- **Title cards:** Bottom fade, text anchors at bottom, face fully visible
- **Filming:** "Document the journey, not perfection" — candid over staged
- **Colour in content:** Draw from the brand palette — lavender, forest, cream
